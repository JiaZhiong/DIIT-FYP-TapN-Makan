Tap N' Makan is a web-based food ordering and delivery platform. It was developed as a group final year project with two other coursemates from my diploma studies in Information Technology under the supervision of Ms. Chik Soon Wai. The platform is developed on WordPress and hosted on the Internet through CPanel services. Google Maps API integration was introduced to help route deliveries to the user's current or specified location.
